How to Apply to Top Universities for Higher Education in Australia

In Australia, international students can apply for higher education in three sessions.

  • February Intake: You have to apply to your preferred university by December to begin the first semester. Apply February Intake
  • June/July Intake: You must apply in early May to start the second semester at the Australian university. Apply Now
  • September to November Intake: September to November Intakes offers specific programs to international students during these months, Depending on the University. Apply Today

Spouse Visa

A spouse visa is a term that refers to an immigrant visa for the spouse. It is generally only for people with a temporary Partner visa (subclass 820). Australian spouse visa application takes up to 14 to 28 months based on the category.

The applicant of this spouse visa or their partner has to be an Australia PR holder/citizen/eligible New Zealand citizen or permanent resident. To get a spouse visa you need some documents:

  • Not Hold an Application Refused
  • Not Hold Certain Regional Visas
  • Be in Australia When You Apply
  • Have a Sponsor
  • Be the Right Age
  • Relationship Requirements
  • Health Requirements
  • Character Requirement
  • No Debt to the Australian Government
  • Best Interests of the Child.
